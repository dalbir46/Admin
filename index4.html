Ex 1.2 , 2 (Method 1)

Find the LCM and HCF of the following pairs of integers and verify that
\( \text{LCM} \times \text{HCF} = \text{product of the two numbers} \).

(i) 26 and 91

\[ \begin{array}{r|r} 2 & 26 \\ \hline 13 & 13 \\ \hline & 1 \end{array} \]
\[ \begin{array}{r|r} 7 & 91 \\ \hline 13 & 13 \\ \hline & 1 \end{array} \]

\(26 = 2 \times 13 = 2^1 \times 13^1\)

\(91 = 7 \times 13 = 7^1 \times 13^1\)

\(\text{H.C.F} = \text{Product of smallest power of each common prime factor}\)

\(= 13\)

\(\text{L.C.M} = \text{Product of greatest power of each prime factor}\)

\(= 2 \times 13 \times 7 = 182\)
